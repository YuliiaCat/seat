About the Game

"Seat Jam" is a captivating puzzle game that turns the art of seat arrangement into a thrilling challenge. Players dive into diverse environments, arranging seats in various venues from cozy cafes to grand theaters. Each level presents unique obstacles and goals, requiring strategic thinking and creativity. With hundreds of levels, the game progressively introduces more complex arrangements and special items, keeping the gameplay fresh and engaging.

How to Play

Drag and drop chairs and tables to match the floor plan. Complete each level by fulfilling the specific arrangement criteria. Use power-ups to rearrange or remove items. Overcome obstacles like immovable structures. Achieve the goals within a limitednumber of moves. Unlock new furniture and decoration themes as you progress. Participate in time-limited events for special rewards. Challenge your friends for the top spot in leaderboards.
