FAQ

  • Can I play Seat Jam offline?

    Yes, the game can be played without an internet connection.

  • How do I unlock more themes?

    Progress through levels or purchase them in the store.

  • Are there any in-app purchases?

    Yes, but they are optional and not required to complete the game.

  • What do I do if I get stuck on a level?

    You can use hints or power-ups to help you out.

  • How can I reset my progress?

    You can reset your progress from the settings menu.

  • Can I play against others?

    Yes, there are leaderboards and social features to compete against friends.
